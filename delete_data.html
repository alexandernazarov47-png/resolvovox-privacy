ResolvoVox — Data Deletion Instructions

ResolvoVox stores all your data locally on your device. We do not store any of your data on our servers. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time using one of the methods below.

Method 1: Delete History Inside the App

  1. Open ResolvoVox
  2. Go to History
  3. Delete individual letters by swiping or using the delete button
  4. This removes the stored letter text, analysis results, deadlines, and draft replies for the selected items

Method 2: Clear All App Data (Complete Deletion)

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Apps (or Applications)
  3. Find and tap ResolvoVox
  4. Tap Storage
  5. Tap Clear Data (or Clear Storage)

This removes all data stored by the app, including letter history, analysis results, your API key, and all preferences.

Method 3: Uninstall the App

Uninstalling ResolvoVox from your device will automatically delete all locally stored data.
