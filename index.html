Privacy Policy

ResolvoVox — Effective date: April 10, 2026

ResolvoVox (“the App”) is developed and maintained by an independent developer (“we”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and what choices you have.

1. What the App Does

ResolvoVox helps you understand official German-language letters. You scan or photograph a letter, the App recognizes the text on your device, then sends the recognized text to the OpenAI API for analysis. The result—a plain-language explanation, suggested actions, deadlines, and a draft reply—is shown and stored locally on your device.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers. The App does not have a backend server operated by us. There are no user accounts, no registration, and no analytics or advertising SDKs.

3. Camera & Photo Library

The App requests access to your camera and photo library solely to capture or select images of letters for text recognition. Images are processed on-device by Google ML Kit; they are not uploaded to any server. Only the resulting text is sent to OpenAI for analysis.

4. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API — Recognized letter text is sent over an encrypted HTTPS connection to OpenAI’s servers for analysis. OpenAI’s use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. When using the API with your own key, OpenAI states that API data is not used to train their models by default.
  • Google ML Kit (Text Recognition) — Text recognition runs entirely on your device. No image data leaves the device through ML Kit. Google’s ML Kit privacy information is available here.
  • Google Fonts — The App may download font files from Google’s servers. This involves a standard HTTPS request; Google’s Privacy Policy applies to those requests.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • All letter history and your API key are stored locally on your device using the platform’s standard storage mechanisms.
  • Data transmitted to OpenAI travels over TLS-encrypted HTTPS.
  • You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing the App’s data in your device settings or by using the in-app history management features.
  • The App provides an export/import feature for your letter history; exported files are saved locally and you control where they are shared.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

  • Access & deletion: Since all data is stored on your device, you have full control. Clear the app data or uninstall the App to remove everything.
  • OpenAI data: For data sent to OpenAI, refer to OpenAI’s Privacy Policy for your rights regarding data they process.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
